Record of Olympics 2016

Summary of the Games

The 2016 Summer Olympics, officially known as the Games of the XXXI Olympiad, were held in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, from August 5 to August 21, 2016. A total of 11,303 athletes from 206 countries participated in 306 events across 28 sports.

The United States topped the medal table with a total of 121 medals, including 46 gold medals. Great Britain finished second with 67 medals, and China finished third with 51 medals.
